Welcome to your home away from home!

Close your eyes and take a deep breath.
Imagine waking up and before you can even open your eyes, you hear the waves crashing and smell the salty air of the Aegean Sea. You hear a knock on your door, and it’s an amazing Grecian breakfast being served to you in bed. You eat, put your bathing suit on, and are at the pool or the beach looking out at the crystal blue water. You have lunch on your balcony as the sun warms your skin, and then head out to do a little bit of shopping at the local stores. That night, you have a wonderful Mediterranean dinner, and then spend the night dancing.

This doesn’t have to be a dream. At Bella Agapi Bed and Breakfast, we can help you make this a reality, and a vacation you won’t soon forget.

View our unique rooms and their breathtaking views in our gallery, drool over our infamous menu, and read reviews from other vacationers! After you’re done, make sure to go to our reservations tab to book your dream vacation!
